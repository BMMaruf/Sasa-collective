We are passionate about travel and exploration, but without our world there is no travel and without it's people there is no discovery. This means we’re also passionate about sustainability. We believe that we can all make a contribution to sustain our planet and its people, so that future generations will have the chance to share our passion too.

What motivates us?

Who are we?

A couple of Aussies who love to travel, love our planet and love the idea of helping our humble home. We feel it’s time to make a change, and it starts with you. We hope that through our simple model we are able to make a difference.

To create environmentally friendly, ethically sourced and sustainably produced bamboo toothbrushes, with a unique style and distinctive feel. Our priority is to bring you a sustainable and stylish product, to keep you and our home smiling. We want to keep sasa Brush affordable and ecologically sound, so we try to eliminate as many costs and as much unnecessary packaging as we can. This means you’ll receive two brushes with each delivery, eliminating the use of excess envelopes, packaging and transportation.
